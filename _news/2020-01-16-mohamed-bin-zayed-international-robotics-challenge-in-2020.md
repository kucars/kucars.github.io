The MBZIRC is an international robotics competition, to be held every two years with total prize and team sponsorship of USD5 million.  KU and KUCARS are the main organizers of the competition. The second edition of MBZIRC will take place in February 2020. More information about MBZIRC 2020 can be found at: [www.mbzirc.com](http://www.mbzirc.com)

This collaborative project between KU and NYU explores neuromorphic hardware that mimics neural architecture and functioning of the human brain. Focus areas include development of neuromorphic chips, algorithm optimization for neuromorphic systems, applications in AI and robotics, and interdisciplinary research combining neuroscience, computer science, and electrical engineering.

This project investigates autonomous aerial drone-based inspection of critical infrastructure and indoor farms, such as concrete containment at nuclear power plants, railway tracks, oil and gas pipelines, and greenhouses. Regular inspection and early problem detection of critical facilities allow for greater safety and efficiency.

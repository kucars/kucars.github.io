Unmanned Aerial Vehicles can play a crucial role in precipitation enhancement by helping identify cloud regions suitable for seeding and performing cloud seeding. This framework establishes testing, validation, and enhancement frameworks for assessing UAV performance for cloud seeding in the UAE context, in collaboration with the National Center of Meteorology.
